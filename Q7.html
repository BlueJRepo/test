A    The JMS connector support both sending and receiving of JMS message over the protocol determined by the JMS provider.

B    To receive messages into the Mule application, the JMS provider initiates a network connection to the JMS connector and
            pushes messages along this connection.

C    To complete sending a JMS message, the JMS connector must establish a network connection with the JMS message recipient.

D    The AMQP protocol can be used by the JMS connector to portably establish connections to various types of JMS providers.
